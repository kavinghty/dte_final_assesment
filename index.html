ROSMINI COLLEGE

Rosmini College offers a Catholic education to young men on Auckland’s North Shore, helping them to become well-rounded individuals that strive to reach their full academic potential. Students are encouraged to participate and excel in sporting and cultural opportunities, and their education is grounded in the philosophy of the school’s founder - to be young men of dignity, integrity, fairness, charity and justice.

Rosmini College is centred around good leadership, sound discipline, excellence in standards expected of all, and a focus on boys’ learning. Rosmini is one of Auckland’s leading achievers, with a proud history of academic and sporting prowess. Technology, music and art also flourish at Rosmini, and we aim to deliver boys a wide range of learning opportunities while maintaining a commitment to enhancing leadership and community service awareness.
